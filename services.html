WHAT TO DO WITH THE ELECTRICAL WASTE AND SCRAP METAL?

Electronic waste, in the form of smaller devices, can usually be disposed of at specialist retailers such as Media Markt or Saturn. Larger appliances such as refrigerators and washing machines can be dropped off at recycling centers. If you buy a new device, the old device can be taken back by the dealer. Scrap metal can be taken to recycling centers or to a scrap dealer. Please feel free to contact us - we will be happy to help you.
